Bharat that is India is a country located in South Asia.
It is a country with diverse landscapes, languages, traditions, cultural heritages etc. From mountains to oceans, from deserts to forests. India is the seventh largest country by area and the most populous country as of June 2023. It got it's independence from the British empire on 15th August,1947.
